About D.S PLASTERING

We design & build functional, enjoyable, and inspiring spaces.

I’m a plasterer based in Kent with 10 years experience. Always a professionally clean job, with fair prices. We can do internal or external work, I am happy to help with any job, no matter how big or small. Each job is completed to a high standard, always making sure that every customer is happy with the service that is provided.

Connecting with the customer is one of the biggest aims during the job to make sure that they are happy and getting the result they want because let’s be honest, it’s you house right.
